Moving Average for the Global data and City data.¶

Now that I could extract data from a database, let's talk through the next step of completing the project: moving averages.
Moving averages are used to smooth out data to make it easier to observe long term trends and not get lost in daily fluctuations
Using a moving average, you can both smooth out the daily volatility and allow you to observe the long term trend.

Moving Average with Python:¶

Simple, cumulative, and exponential moving averages with Pandas
The moving average is also known as rolling mean.

The easiest way to calculate the simple moving average is by using the pandas.Series.rolling method.

In [14]:
